About Archetype

Revolutionizing the electrical product safety certification process.

Simplifying Electrical Product Certification

Archetype bridges the gap between manufacturers and certification bodies, revolutionizing the electrical product safety certification process. We prioritize efficiency—understanding that for manufacturers, time is more valuable than money.

Our Mission

We streamline the certification journey by helping manufacturers identify the specific standards they should be asking certification bodies about, reducing confusion and saving valuable time.

What We Do

  • Standards Guidance: We help manufacturers pinpoint exactly which standards apply to their products, so they can ask the right questions and avoid costly delays.
  • Proactive Updates: We empower manufacturers to stay ahead by tracking changes to the standards their certifications are based on.
  • Certification Body Collaboration: Our guidance on working effectively with certification bodies ensures products receive certifications on time—crucial for maintaining production schedules.
  • Certification Directory: Manufacturers can post their certifications on our website, creating a centralized platform where customers can find certified components for their own compliance needs.

Why It Matters

One of the most common reasons for missed production dates is the difficulty in locating appropriately certified components. By providing a searchable directory of certified products, we help manufacturers:

  • Reduce the risk of production delays
  • Accelerate time-to-market for their products
  • Support their customers in meeting compliance requirements efficiently
  • Create a more interconnected ecosystem that benefits all parties

Our goal is to minimize the financial losses associated with missed certification timelines while enhancing operational efficiency for every customer we serve.
